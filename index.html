My weekly Data-Viz inspirations

Once a week, i'm used to get inspirations for my datavizualization work. Here is my 3 favourite spots to get some ideas !

1. Tableau public gallery

Tableau Public gallery is one of my favourite source of inspirations. Tableau's best users seem to master artwork datavisualizations. They are equally good artists and analysts.

To enjoy even more vizzes and learn there best pratices, you can see the Iron VIz challenge finalists in live on this YouTube video

Congratulations to the winner, Lisa !

2. National Geographic

National Geographic always as great inforgraphics that inspires me.

Reading there magazines is a real source of pleasure for the eyes and we learn a lot about many themes.

3. Twitter #dataviz

Twitter, as well as Instagram or Pinterest, are a good place to find the best dataviz and follow your favorite authors.
